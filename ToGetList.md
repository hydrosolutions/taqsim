
# ToGetList

- Geometry (cross-sections, length, slope, …)
- Capacity
- Discharge measurements/MoW?
- Canal condition (infiltration, losses, …)
- Velocity data
- Roughness

## Hydroworks (Ravadkhoza, Damkhodza, AkKaraDarya, Narpay, Karmana)
- Hydraulic information
- Operating rules/flow control/water distribution (weirs, gates & locks)
- Discharge measurements/MoW?

## Reservoirs (Kattakurgan, AkDarya)
- Operation rules
- Inflow/outflow MoW
- Water level-volume relationship - optional remote sensing
- Area-height relationship - optional remote sensing

## Water Demand
- Industrial demands
- Municipal demands/population size, e.g. Samarkand, Navoi, Bukahra drinking water pipeline
- Agricultural demands (rules for water distribution/opening and closing gates)

- +/- 3 good scenarios
- Crop, population, climate scenarios
